ABOUT FYIND

FYIND is a Professional Service that helps organisations in strengthening their supply chain of services with respect to its Information Technology and Business Process Services needs. We bring an extensive network of reliable and capable Technology suppliers in India. Our experts can make a recommendation of three to five suppliers that may best align to your needs. The recommendation will take into consideration your industry, nature of services, skills required and the scale and duration of the relationship.

Our team has extensive experience in Next Gen solutions including Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning, Data Science, Digital, Cloud, Blockchain and Internet of Things (IOT).

In a nutshell, our goal is to help you leverage the strength of the skills and expertise available in India.

We offer up to 2 hours of consultation without any cost. Feel free to reach out to us at service@fyind.io
